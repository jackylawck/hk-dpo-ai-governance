# HK DPO GenAI Governance Sandbox (香港數字政策辦公室指引合規沙盒)

An enterprise GenAI compliance workstation built 100% deterministically per the HK Digital Policy Office (DPO) "Generative AI Technical and Application Guideline V1.1" (Dec 2025)[cite: 1, 2].
本系統為一套企業級生成式人工智能合規工作站，核心邏輯 100% 依據香港數字政策辦公室 (DPO) 於 2025 年 12 月發布之《香港生成式人工智能技術及應用指引》V1.1 進行決定性編碼[cite: 1, 2]。

### ⚠️ 範圍與免責聲明 (Scope and Limitation of Liability)

* **Strict Alignment (嚴格對齊基準)：** This system is deterministically coded based exclusively on the HK DPO "Generative AI Technical and Application Guideline V1.1" (Dec 2025)[cite: 1, 2]. 
  本系統核心邏輯 100% 依據香港數字政策辦公室 (DPO) 發布之《香港生成式人工智能技術及應用指引》V1.1 (2025年12月) 進行決定性編碼[cite: 1, 2]。

* **Out of Scope (超出範圍界定)：** It does not evaluate cross-jurisdictional frameworks (e.g., EU AI Act, GDPR), general employment ordinances, or non-AI related corporate policies. Detailed boundary conditions can be found in **[Technological Limitations](./docs/limitations.md)**.
  本系統不評估跨司法管轄區框架（如歐盟 AI 法案、GDPR）、一般僱傭法例，或與人工智能無關之企業內部政策。詳細的邊界條件請參閱 **[技術限制說明](./docs/limitations.md)**。

* **Not Legal Advice (非法律意見)：** The outputs generated by this diagnostic engine are for internal governance reference and risk triage only. They do not constitute formal legal or professional compliance advice.
  本診斷引擎生成之結果僅供企業內部管治參考與初步風險定級，絕對不構成正式之法律或專業合規意見。

### 🛡️ 治理設計理念 (Governance Philosophy)

* **Strict Boundary Control (嚴格邊界控制)：** To ensure compliance assessment precision, the logic base of this system is 100% strictly locked to the text of the DPO "Generative AI Guideline"[cite: 1, 2].
  為確保合規評估的精準度，本系統的邏輯基準 100% 嚴格鎖定於數字政策辦公室《生成式人工智能技術及應用指引》文本內容。

* **Refusal to Answer Outside Scope (邊界外拒絕作答)：** Queries falling outside the scope of the "Generative AI Guideline" will be rejected to prevent the creation of "Compliance Illusions".
  若查詢內容超出《生成式人工智能技術及應用指引》的適用範圍，系統將拒絕提供任何衍生性建議，以避免造成合規幻覺 (Compliance Illusion)。
