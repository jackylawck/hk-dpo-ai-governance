# Contributing to HK DPO GenAI Governance Sandbox

We welcome contributions that align strictly with the official texts of the Hong Kong Digital Policy Office (DPO)[cite: 1, 2]. 
我們歡迎所有嚴格基於香港數字政策辦公室（DPO）官方指引文本的貢獻與反饋[cite: 1, 2]。

## Contribution Guidelines (貢獻守則)
1. **No Copyrighted Redistribution (嚴禁分發版權材料):** Do not upload copyrighted standard PDFs (e.g., ISO standards, paywalled regulatory texts) to this repository.
   請勿將任何受版權保護的標準全文 PDF（如 ISO/IEC 42001 標準原文）上傳至本公開倉庫中。
2. **Determinism First (決定性優先):** Any logic update to `app.py` must be based on explicit paragraphs of official guidelines, not subjective assumptions.
   任何對 `app.py` 邏輯的更新，必須基於官方指引的明確條文，而非主觀假設。
