# Jacky Law Human-Centred AI Governance Stack (人本 AI 管治五層架構)

This repository adopts the **Jacky Law Human-Centred AI Governance Stack**, a holistic framework integrating Corporate HR leadership, Mediation expertise, and ISO/IEC 42001 Auditing standards to govern generative AI deployments.
本專案採用 **Jacky Law 人本 AI 管治五層架構**。這是一套整合了企業人力資源領導力、專業調解機制與 ISO/IEC 42001 審計標準的全局性框架，用於對齊與實施生成式 AI 的部署管治。

---

## 🧱 The 5-Layer Stack (五層架構架構)

### 1. Purpose & Accountability (業務目的與問責)
* **EN:** Defines clear business objectives, identifies system owners, establishes formal authorization, and ensures board-level oversight before any AI implementation.
* **ZH (繁中):** 釐清業務目的、明確系統責任人、落實正式授權，並在 AI 部署前確保董事會層級的實質監督。

### 2. People & Workforce Impact (員工與組織影響)
* **EN:** Evaluates impact on recruitment, bias, workforce transformation, employee monitoring, and redress mechanisms (incorporating Mediation principles to resolve AI-workplace disputes).
* **ZH (繁中):** 評估對人才招聘、公平性、員工監察、職能重塑之影響，並引入調解機制處理因 AI 決策引發的勞資爭議。

### 3. Data & Privacy Controls (數據與隱私控制)
* **EN:** Enforces data minimisation, pseudonymisation, storage limitation, and strict access controls (aligned with PDPO DPPs and DPO's 14-point checklists)[cite: 1, 2].
* **ZH (繁中):** 實施資料最小化、去識別化、保留期限限制與嚴格存取控制（深度對齊《私隱條例》DPPs 與數字辦 14 項自查表）[cite: 1, 2]。

### 4. Model & Decision Assurance (模型與決策保障)
* **EN:** Mitigates algorithmic bias, ensures local explainability, maintains robust "Human-in-the-loop" (HITL) oversight, and conducts periodic factual validation to prevent hallucinations.
* **ZH (繁中):** 防範演算法偏見、確保局部可解釋性、維持實質性的「人在環中 (HITL)」人為監督，並定期進行事實核實以抑制模型幻覺。

### 5. Evidence & Resolution (審計證據與事故響應)
* **EN:** Implements immutable audit logging, executes formal AI incident response plans, and establishes clear resolution paths for non-compliance.
* **ZH (繁中):** 部署不可篡改的審計日誌、執行正式的 AI 事故應變與資料外洩處理計劃，並為違規事件建立明確的補救解決路徑。
